Downtown Dog Lounge

A seamless boarding service for the pooches' parents

Overview

I started to concern if there is any users that have the same experience like me.

Duration: Mar 2018 - May 2018
Roles: UX Designer
Tools: Sketch, InVision

1. Research


The Challenge

As many of the dog parents, I was moving into Seattle and looking for a boarding for my dog.I was introduced the Downtown Dog Lounge by my friend and it was closed to where I studied. But when I tried to look up their service and to check if I can put the dog to their place, I have to create an account first. Before making the final decision, the system asked me to fill in my credit card information and this is what held me back...

Learn from People

To make sure it a real problem, I conducted the contexual interview with the potential users and here are some of the quotes from them:

"Wondering if it is possible to book reservation through online from checking room availability to checkout"

"I can call them to book the room but it takes forever to confirm all the information"

"It'd be great if I can get helped with some inputs in the long sign-in form. "
