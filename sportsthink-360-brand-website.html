Sportsthink-360 Brand Website

To build up the brand identity for a youth athlete consulting company

Overview

Sportsthink 360 aims to provide a professional athlete sharing community that benefits the young athletes’ development through the well-structured programs. This is another side project I worked by applying what I learned from school to design the brand new website. Note: The placeholder images of the final protosite are for interal usage only. Photos credit: TCBOOST.

2017 - Present
Roles: Researcher / UX Designer
Tools: Illustrator / Sketch

Research

Research Process including the competitor analysis and collect the feedback from the prospect users.

Competitor Analysis
Though SportsThink360 has no existing competitors in South Africa, there are a few competitors that offer identical services to certain targets audiences in US and can be a good example to understand the whole industry. While the RedLine Athletics provides training programs to help young athletes ages 8-18 achieving their goals and maximizing their potential, EXOS targets more broadly from kids to adults, from normal people to athletes. In addition to the training program, the EXOS delivers sufficient sport-related blog post for the whole community which the SportsThink360 is targeting to build with its strong connection with the expert.
