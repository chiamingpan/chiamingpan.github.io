Sportsthink-360 Brand Website

An website for the youth athletes consulting company to build the brand identity

Overview

Sportsthink 360 consulting company aims to provide the professional services that benefit the young athletes' development through the well-structured programs and sharing community. This is another side project I worked on by applying the skills of HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and WordPress I learned from school. Note: The placeholder images of the protosite are for interal usage only. Photos credit: TCBOOST.

Goal:
Design Sportsthink 360 official website to raise the awareness of the brand in South Africa.

Duration: 2017 - Present
Roles: UX Designer, Front-end Developer
Tools: Illustrator, Sketch

1. Research

Competitor Analysis

Though SportsThink360 has no existing competitors in South Africa, there are a few competitors that offer identical services for certain targets audiences in US and considered the good examples for us to understand the whole industry. While the RedLine Athletics provides training programs to help young athletes aged 8-18 achieving their goals and maximizing their potential, EXOS targets more broadly from kids to adults, from the public to athletes. In addition to the training programs, the EXOS delivers sufficient sport-related blog posts for the community which is one of the focuses that SportsThink360 targets to highlight.
